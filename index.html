How it works?

🪨, 📰, and ✂️ icons are rendered and move randomly across the screen. When two icons meet, they change according to the rules of the animation:

  1. 📰 wins 🪨
  2. 🪨 wins ✂️
  3. ✂️ wins 📰

About Rock, Paper and Scissors

It is a simple hand game played between two people where each player forms one of three hand signs: rock, paper, or scissors. The objective is to choose a sign that defeats the other player's sign based on a set of rules. It is typically played in a best-of-three format and can be played by anyone, anywhere, without any special equipment.
